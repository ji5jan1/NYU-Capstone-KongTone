# KONGTONE

<div align="center"><i>NYU MS in Publishing Capstone Project</i></div><br>

KongTone is a Cantonese learning website. The naming had multiple layers: it sounds like 'Canton', consists Hong 'Kong', and means 'to make sense' in Cantonese. 

This project means a lot for me personally. As the only person from Hong Kong, even the only person who speaks Chinese in my program, I have been struggling to be understood. I felt lonely, because I could not find anyone who has a similar background as I do.

In the final semester, I took the Capstone course as an opportunity to share my pride in my cultural background. Merging with the industry knowledge I learned in the program, KongTone came to life. When I sumbitted the project, I felt a sense of accomplishment, as well as a huge wave of emptiness. This two-year journey has ended, and it's time to move on.

This is not a perfect proposal, but at least the best I can accomplish at this stage. There's still a long way to go...!

### Something about the project...
- Platform: digital (website & web app)
- Content arrangement: multimedia, interactive courses
- Target audience: Cantonese as Second Language (CSL) learners, second-/ third-generation immigrants, diaspora communities
- Revenue streams: in-app currency sales, display ads, native ads

### Skills & tech involved
- Figma
- UI & prototype design
- Linguistics
- Entrepreneurship
- Market research
- Marketing
- Accounting
- Financial projection

# 港通

<div align="center"><i>紐約大學出版學碩士畢業專題</i></div><br>

「港通」是一個廣東話學習平台。它的命名有多重意思：讀音似「廣東」，又似「講（得）通」，也有「港」元素。

這份論文對我來說意義重大。作為在系上唯一的香港人、甚至是唯一會講中文的人，我一直都覺得有一種不被理解的感覺。我很難找到跟我背景相近的人可以圍爐，可說是孤單不已。

在最後一個學期，我終於可以透過畢業專題的機會來好好分享我的文化，同時融合了我這一年多學習到的業界知識，最終製作了這份畢業作品。最後繳交作品時，除了成功感外，一陣空虛感油然而生。因為這代表我這兩年的學習歷程已經結束，是時候要進入人生下一階段了。

作為學生作品，我深知尚有不少進步空間，還望各位讀者多多包涵。

### 少許背景⋯⋯
- 平台：數位（網站、網路應用程式）
- 內容形式：多媒體互動課程

- 營利模式：平台代幣銷售、數位廣告、原生廣告
